‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does ScanBites work?

Simply scan a food product's barcode for instant FREE analysis, or take a photo of ingredient panels. Most analysis happens locally on your device with no costs!

Is my data private?

Yes! ScanBites is completely private. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Most analysis happens entirely on your device.

Do I need an API key?

No! The app works great without any setup. 90% of analysis is completely FREE using local intelligence. You can optionally add an OpenAI API key from OpenAI's website for premium OCR on complex ingredient photos.

What's the difference between Free and Premium OCR?

FREE OCR uses smart local methods and works great for most ingredient photos. Premium AI OCR uses OpenAI for the most complex or blurry photos. The app always tries FREE first and gives you the choice!

How do I set up my dietary preferences?

Go to Settings in the app and configure your dietary restrictions, allergens, and preferences. This helps personalize your ingredient analysis.

üîß Troubleshooting

Camera not working?

Make sure you've granted camera permissions in your device settings. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera > ScanBites.

OCR not working?

Try the FREE OCR first - it works great for most photos! If you need premium OCR for complex images, add an OpenAI API key in app settings and ensure you have an active internet connection.

Barcode scanning issues?

Make sure the barcode is clearly visible and well-lit. Try cleaning your camera lens and holding the device steady.
